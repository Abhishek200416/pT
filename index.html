About Patrinova

Patrinova connects the global Indian diaspora with local artisans, MSMEs, and sellers across India, offering high-quality products like Puja Samagri, traditional attires, handicrafts, and more. Our mission is to bring the diaspora closer to their roots with the finest of Indian culture, delivered straight to your doorstep.
